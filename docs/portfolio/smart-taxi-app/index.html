Smart Taxi ME

Built at Aiia-solutions.Smart Taxi or Lyft of Arab world ! is an ingenious application architected and conceived in the region, in order to best serve the specific needs & requirements of taxi users in the GCC Countries in particular as well as the Arab world in general.

Features

  • Select pickup location
  • Taxi Info
  • Live tracking of taxi driver
  • Call your taxi driver
  • notification for driver status
